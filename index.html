Privacy Policy — Priorities

Effective date: 16 July 2026

Priorities is a party game made by BlueAlder. This policy describes how the app handles your information. The short version: your data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.

Data we collect

None. Priorities does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app:

  • has no user accounts and requires no sign-in;
  • contains no advertising and no analytics or tracking of any kind;
  • makes no network connections.

Data stored on your device

Game history (player names you type in, the items you rank, and scores) and your sound preference are stored locally on your device only. Deleting a game in the History screen, clearing all history, or uninstalling the app permanently removes this data.

Your device's standard backup service (for example Google's device backup on Android or iCloud device backup on iOS) may include the app's data in your own device backups, under your control and your platform account's terms.

Sharing and export

The History screen has an export feature that creates a backup file of your game history. This file is only created and shared when you explicitly choose to, using your device's share sheet, to a destination you pick. The app never sends this file anywhere on its own.

Children

Priorities does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated and the effective date above revised.

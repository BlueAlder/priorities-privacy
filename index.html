Privacy Policy — Priorities

Effective date: 19 July 2026

Priorities is a party game made by BlueAlder. This policy describes how the app handles your information. The short version: your data stays on your device and in your own personal cloud backup — it never reaches us or anyone else unless you choose to share it.

Data we collect

None. Priorities does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app:

  • has no user accounts and requires no sign-in;
  • contains no advertising and no analytics or tracking of any kind;
  • makes no network connections of its own. The only data that leaves the device is the backup copy described below, which your device's operating system stores in your own personal cloud storage.

Data stored on your device

Game history (player names you type in, the items you rank, and scores) and your sound preference are stored locally on your device. So that your history can survive deleting and reinstalling the app, a copy of your game history is also kept in your own personal cloud storage: your private iCloud storage on iOS, or your Google backup on Android. That storage belongs to your platform account — BlueAlder has no access to it and no servers of its own. Deleting a game in the History screen or clearing all history also removes it from that backup copy.

Sharing and export

The History screen has an export feature that creates a backup file of your game history. This file is only created and shared when you explicitly choose to, using your device's share sheet, to a destination you pick. The app never sends this file anywhere on its own.

Children

Priorities does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated and the effective date above revised.
